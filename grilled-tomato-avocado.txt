Sun-Dried Tomato and Avocado Grilled Cheese

Makes: 1 sandwich
â€‹Ready in: 15 minutes

INGREDIENTS

1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil, divided
2 slices whole-wheat bread
2 slices sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 avocado, sliced
6 sun-dried tomatoes

DIRECTIONS

1. Heat a pan over medium-low heat. Brush 1/2 teaspoon olive oil on one side of a bread slice and place in the pan oil side down.

2. Top with cheese, avocado, tomatoes, and remaining slice of bread.

3. Lightly brush second slice of bread with remaining 1/2 teaspoon oil. Cook for about 2 minutes or until bread turns golden brown and cheese begins to melt.

4. Flip, and cook on the other side until bread is toasted and cheese is completely melted.
